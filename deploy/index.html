The Great In-Between

– A Mongol Rally Map Journal –

In the summer of 2014, three friends drove from England to Mongolia in a 1.1-liter Suzuki Alto hatchback. Each day, they documented their experiences with photos, videos, and written journal entries. This map retraces their journey, one day at a time.
