Cities of the Dispossessed

– A Tour of the World's Largest Refugee Camps –

Around the world, nearly four million refugees reside in planned or self-settled camps. This interactive map, created in commemoration of World Humanitarian Day (August 19th), identifies the ten most populous refugee camps in the world, drawing attention to the plight—and the resilience—of the planet's most vulnerable populations, and to the organizations that work tirelessly to protect them.
